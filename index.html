About Me

My name is Kyle Sisson. I was born and raised in Southern California. I attended California Polytechnic State University in San Luis Obispo, CA where I earned an undergraduate degree in Business Administration with a concentration in Entrepreneurship. After graduating from college, I opened a restaurant with my family in Culver City called Primal Kitchen. Unfortunately, we recently had to close Primal Kitchen due to unforseen circumstances. Now, with the freedom to start a career from scratch, I have decided to embark on a journey to learn web-development. I am currently enrolled in a coding bootcamp where I hope to learn the skills necessary to be competitive in the industry.
